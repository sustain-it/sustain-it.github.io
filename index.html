Sustain It is fully committed to recycling waste and keeping it out of landfill. With new ideas and innovation in technology, we can work towards a better environment for future generations.

Sustain It!

Are you interested in being more sustainable? Sustain It is a brand new app for your phone that includes everything you need to recyle more effectively

  • Information on recycling better
    • Electronics waste recycling
    • Plastics recycling
    • Organic Waste recycling
  • News and tips from your community
  • Map integration to find nearby recycling centers via postcode or GPS location
  • A community store where you can purchase products made with recycled materials
