Sustain It
Mission statement here Team 15 aims to improve participation in recycling through technology innovation

The Solution

Team 15 will build a solution that utilizes technology to bring together innovative ideas in information delivery and engagement to increase participation and awareness in recycling.

The Growth Opportunity

Businesses have many growth opportunities. SustainIT leads the market by providing waste lines to other competitors to keep them doing business. The opportunities for growth are:

  • Competition: Web and mobile apps create competition and awareness among those who participate in the program, resulting in the influx of more of our raw materials into the facility.
  • Growth: This business is related to technology that enables us to expand our business from outside Victoria to other Australian states.
  • Fun and reward points: The apps used by the company are a kind of fun activity for users, who participate in the collection of plastic waste for a sustainable environment and create a circular economy. Bonus points earned by users can be used for all types of purchases at participating business stores. This will allow you to use our technology and stop participating in the business. Therefore, businesses grow in most Australian communities.
