Sustain It

Our Mission

Sustain It is fully committed to recycling waste and keeping it out of landfill. With new ideas and innovation in technology, we can work towards a better environment for future generations.

The Solution

Our solution will be a smartphone application/app called “Sustain It” that aims to include the following features:

  • Information regarding recycling
    • Electronics waste recycling
    • Plastics recycling
    • Organic Waste recycling
  • “News” or blog feed with updates on recycling policy and information/articles about ideas on how to recycle more effectively.
  • Map integration to find nearby recycling centers via postcode or GPS location
  • Store/vendor for purchasing recyclable we will used recycled materials to create products which can be purchased in a store.
